With Intention

Welcome

With Intention is a Chrome extension that helps you browse the web more intentionally.

Adding your first website

Navigate to a website you want to browse intentionally and open the extension via the extension menu in the top-right of your browser window. Add the website.

Browsing with intention

After the website has been added, the extension will ask you about your intention every time you visit the website.

Use this opportunity to jot down your intention or leave the website.

Managing your websites

You can manage your websites from the extension's options page.

Tip

If you want to use the extension only at certain times, you can set a time period in the options.
